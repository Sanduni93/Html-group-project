Laboratory Service

Our laboratories handle and prepare medical specimens/samples for analysis, process and analyze these samples then deliver the test results/findings to our patients and their requesting doctors. Our fully licensed Medical Laboratory offers a wide range of services in the following key sections:

  • Hematology & Immunohaematology
  • Biochemistry
  • Special Chemistry (Immunology, Hormone, Therapeutic Drug Monitoring)
  • Serology
  • Body Fluids, Stool & Urine Analysis
  • Microbiology

HEMATOLOGY & IMMUNOHAEMATOLOGY

This section analyses patients' blood, looking for abnormalities in red blood cells, white blood cells, platelets, and plasma components relating to homeostasis (clotting factors). This section also provides blood and blood products for emergency situations and for transfusion patients who are going for surgery.



Eye Care

Our eye clinic provides consultation, evaluation and treatment of ophthalmological problems. Our compassionate staff and skilled specialists provide a comprehensive array of vision and eye health services. Services range from routine vision screenings and eye care to the diagnosis and treatment of complex and rare eye conditions. Services at our eye clinic are tied up with the Vision Care Optical Services.



ENT Care

Out ENT department provides a full range of clinical services for the diagnosis and treatment of both simple and complicated diseases of the ear, nose, throat in children and adults. We are the only private healthcare providers in Jaffna offering a variety of ENT surgeries with stat of the art technology with minimized post operative pain and other complications.
